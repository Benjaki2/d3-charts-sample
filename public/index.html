Obesity in teens and young adults in The United States

These visualization represents mean percentage values of obese and overweight, males and females, in 2013.

This visualization was created to show how the percentage of obesity in the U.S. fluctuates between adolescence and young adulthood. The visualization suggests that obesity levels rise significantly for women during their college years in comparison to men. As the number of female undergraduates continues to rise... this noticeable increase suggests a correlation between weight gain and the college experience.
